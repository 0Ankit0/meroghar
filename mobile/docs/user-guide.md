## Getting Started

### Installation

**For Android**:

1. Open Google Play Store
2. Search for "MeroGhar"
3. Tap "Install"
4. Open the app after installation

**For iOS**:

1. Open App Store
2. Search for "MeroGhar"
3. Tap "Get"
4. Open the app after installation

### Creating Your Account

1. **Open the MeroGhar app**
2. **Tap "Register"** on the welcome screen
3. **Fill in your details**:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number (with country code)
   - Password (minimum 8 characters)
4. **Tap "Create Account"**
5. **Verify your email** (check your inbox for verification link)
6. **Login** with your credentials

### First-Time Setup

After logging in for the first time:

1. **Complete your profile**:

   - Add profile photo (optional)
   - Update contact information
   - Set language preference

2. **Enable notifications**:

   - Allow push notifications for payment alerts
   - Configure notification preferences

3. **Setup offline mode**:
   - Enable offline access
   - Configure sync preferences

## For Property Owners

As a property owner, you can manage properties, tenants, payments, expenses, and view analytics.

### Managing Properties

#### Adding a New Property

1. **Tap the "+" button** on the home screen
2. **Select "Add Property"**
3. **Fill in property details**:
   - Property Name (e.g., "Green Valley Apartments")
   - Address
   - Total Units
   - Property Type (Apartment, House, Commercial)
   - Description (optional)
4. **Tap "Save"**

#### Editing Property Details

1. **Go to "Properties"** tab
2. **Tap on a property**
3. **Tap the edit icon** (✏️)
4. **Update information**
5. **Tap "Save"**

#### Assigning Intermediaries

If you have a property manager who handles day-to-day operations:

1. **Go to property details**
2. **Tap "Manage Access"**
3. **Tap "Add Intermediary"**
4. **Enter their email** (they must have registered)
5. **Select permissions**:
   - View tenants
   - Create tenants
   - Record payments
   - Manage bills
   - Track expenses
6. **Tap "Assign"**

### Managing Tenants

#### Adding a New Tenant

1. **Select a property**
2. **Tap "Add Tenant"**
3. **Fill in tenant details**:
   - Full Name
   - Email
   - Phone Number
   - Unit Number
   - Move-in Date
   - Monthly Rent (in NPR)
   - Security Deposit
   - Billing Day (1-28)
4. **Tap "Create Tenant"**

The app will automatically create a tenant account and send login credentials to their email.

#### Viewing Tenant Information

1. **Go to "Tenants"** tab
2. **Tap on a tenant** to view:
   - Contact information
   - Lease details
   - Payment history
   - Current balance
   - Documents

#### Deactivating a Tenant

When a tenant moves out:

1. **Go to tenant details**
2. **Tap "Move Out"**
3. **Select move-out date**
4. **Settle final balance**
5. **Tap "Confirm Move Out"**

### Recording Payments

#### Manual Payment Entry

When receiving cash or cheque:

1. **Go to "Payments"** tab
2. **Tap "Record Payment"**
3. **Select tenant**
4. **Enter amount**
5. **Select payment method**:
   - Cash
   - Cheque
   - Bank Transfer
   - Online Payment
6. **Select payment date**
7. **Add notes** (optional)
8. **Tap "Record"**

A receipt will be automatically generated and sent to the tenant's email.

#### Viewing Payment History

1. **Go to "Payments"** tab
2. **Filter by**:
   - Tenant
   - Date range
   - Payment method
   - Status
3. **Tap on a payment** to view receipt

### Managing Bills

#### Creating a Utility Bill

1. **Go to "Bills"** tab
2. **Tap "Add Bill"**
3. **Select property**
4. **Choose bill type**:
   - Electricity
   - Water
   - Gas
   - Internet
   - Maintenance
   - Other
5. **Enter total amount**
6. **Select billing period**
7. **Set due date**
8. **Tap "Create"**

#### Allocating Bills to Tenants

After creating a bill:

1. **Select allocation method**:
   - **Equal Split**: Divide equally among all tenants
   - **By Percentage**: Assign custom percentages
   - **Fixed Amount**: Assign fixed amounts
   - **By Unit Size**: Proportional to unit size
2. **Review allocation** (ensure total = 100%)
3. **Tap "Allocate"**

Tenants will receive notifications about their bill share.

#### Marking Bills as Paid

When a tenant pays their bill share:

1. **Go to bill details**
2. **Find tenant's allocation**
3. **Tap "Mark as Paid"**
4. **Select payment method**
5. **Confirm**

#### Setting Up Recurring Bills

For monthly bills:

1. **Go to "Bills"** tab
2. **Tap "Setup Recurring Bill"**
3. **Configure**:
   - Bill type
   - Base amount
   - Frequency (Monthly, Quarterly)
   - Generation day (e.g., 1st of month)
   - Allocation template
4. **Tap "Save"**

The system will automatically generate and allocate bills on the scheduled day.

### Tracking Expenses

#### Recording an Expense

1. **Go to "Expenses"** tab
2. **Tap "Add Expense"**
3. **Fill in details**:
   - Property
   - Category (Maintenance, Repairs, Insurance, etc.)
   - Amount
   - Date
   - Description
   - Paid by (You or Intermediary)
4. **Upload receipt** (photo or PDF)
5. **Mark as "Requires Approval"** if paid by intermediary
6. **Tap "Save"**

#### Approving Expenses

For intermediary-submitted expenses:

1. **Go to "Expenses"** tab
2. **Filter by "Pending Approval"**
3. **Tap on expense**
4. **Review details and receipt**
5. **Tap "Approve"** or "Reject"\*\*
6. **Add notes** (if rejecting)

### Viewing Analytics

#### Dashboard Overview

The home dashboard shows:

- **Total Properties**
- **Active Tenants**
- **This Month's Collection**
- **Pending Payments**
- **Recent Activities**

#### Financial Analytics

1. **Go to "Analytics"** tab
2. **View charts**:

   - **Rent Collection Trends**: Line chart over 12 months
   - **Payment Status**: Pie chart (Paid, Overdue, Pending)
   - **Expense Breakdown**: Pie chart by category
   - **Revenue vs Expenses**: Bar chart comparison
   - **Property Performance**: Table view

3. **Customize date range**:

   - Tap date range picker
   - Select start and end dates
   - Tap "Apply"

4. **Drill down into details**:
   - Tap any chart element
   - View detailed breakdown

#### Exporting Analytics

1. **Go to Analytics**
2. **Tap "Export" button**
3. **Select format**:
   - Excel (.xlsx)
   - PDF
4. **Choose date range**
5. **Tap "Download"**

File will be saved to your device and can be shared.

### Generating Tax Reports

#### Annual Income Statement

1. **Go to "Reports"** tab
2. **Tap "Tax Reports"**
3. **Select "Annual Income"**
4. **Choose year**
5. **Tap "Generate Report"**
6. **View or export**:
   - Total rental income
   - Breakdown by property
   - Breakdown by month
   - Payment method summary

#### Deductible Expenses Report

1. **Go to "Reports"** → **"Tax Reports"**
2. **Select "Deductible Expenses"**
3. **Choose year**
4. **Generate report** showing:
   - Total deductible expenses
   - Breakdown by category
   - Breakdown by property
   - Non-deductible expenses (separately listed)

#### GST/VAT Report

For Nepal's 13% GST:

1. **Go to "Reports"** → **"Tax Reports"**
2. **Select "GST Report"**
3. **Choose year and quarter**
4. **Generate report** with:
   - Taxable income
   - Output GST (collected from tenants)
   - Input GST (paid on expenses)
   - Net GST payable

#### Financial Reports

**Profit & Loss Statement**:

1. **Go to "Reports"** → **"Financial Reports"**
2. **Select "Profit & Loss"**
3. **Choose period** (Month, Quarter, Year, Custom)
4. **View**:
   - Total revenue
   - Total expenses
   - Net profit
   - Profit margin

**Cash Flow Report**:

1. **Select "Cash Flow"**
2. **Choose period**
3. **View**:
   - Cash inflows (rent, deposits)
   - Cash outflows (expenses, refunds)
   - Net cash flow

#### Sharing Reports

To share with your accountant:

1. **Open any report**
2. **Tap "Share" button**
3. **Set expiration** (1-90 days)
4. **Tap "Generate Link"**
5. **Copy link** and send via email/WhatsApp

The recipient can view the report without needing an account. Link expires automatically.

### Configuring Rent Increments

#### Setting Automatic Rent Increase

1. **Go to tenant details**
2. **Tap "Rent Policy"**
3. **Configure**:
   - Increment Percentage (e.g., 5%)
   - Frequency (e.g., Every 24 months)
   - Next Increment Date
   - Notify Days Before (e.g., 30 days)
4. **Tap "Save"**

The system will:

- Automatically increase rent on the specified date
- Send notification to tenant 30 days before
- Update payment amounts automatically
- Keep history of all rent changes

#### Manual Rent Override

To change rent outside of the policy:

1. **Go to tenant details**
2. **Tap "Override Rent"**
3. **Enter new amount**
4. **Select effective date**
5. **Add reason** (optional)
6. **Tap "Update Rent"**

### Managing Documents

#### Uploading Documents

1. **Go to "Documents"** tab
2. **Tap "Upload Document"**
3. **Select document type**:
   - Lease Agreement
   - Property Deed
   - Insurance Policy
   - Tax Documents
   - Receipts
   - Other
4. **Choose file** (PDF or image)
5. **Associate with**:
   - Property
   - Tenant (optional)
6. **Set expiration date** (for lease agreements)
7. **Tap "Upload"**

#### Document Expiration Reminders

The app will automatically:

- Send notification 30 days before document expiration
- Show warning badge on expired documents
- Remind to upload renewed versions

### Sending Messages

#### Bulk Payment Reminders

1. **Go to "Messages"** tab
2. **Tap "Send Reminder"**
3. **Select tenants** (or filter by "Overdue Payments")
4. **Choose message template**:
   - Payment Reminder
   - Bill Due Reminder
   - Custom Message
5. **Personalize message** (automatic variables: name, amount, due date)
6. **Select channel**:
   - SMS
   - WhatsApp
   - Both
7. **Tap "Send"**

#### Scheduling Messages

1. **Follow steps above**
2. **Instead of "Send", tap "Schedule"**
3. **Select date and time**
4. **Set recurrence** (optional):
   - Daily
   - Weekly
   - Monthly
5. **Tap "Schedule"**

#### Viewing Message History

1. **Go to "Messages"** tab
2. **Filter by**:
   - Tenant
   - Date range
   - Message type
   - Status (Sent, Delivered, Failed)
3. **Tap on message** to view details

## For Intermediaries

As an intermediary (property manager), you handle daily operations for properties assigned to you by owners.

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard shows:

- **Assigned Properties**
- **Total Tenants**
- **Payments Due This Month**
- **Pending Expense Approvals**
- **Today's Tasks**

### Daily Tasks

#### Recording Rent Payments

When a tenant pays rent:

1. **Go to "Tenants"** tab
2. **Find tenant** (or use search)
3. **Tap "Record Payment"**
4. **Enter amount**
5. **Select payment method**
6. **Take photo of receipt** (if cash/cheque)
7. **Tap "Record"**

Receipt is automatically generated and emailed to tenant and owner.

#### Creating Bills

1. **Go to "Bills"** tab
2. **Tap "Add Bill"**
3. **Enter utility bill details**
4. **Upload bill photo**
5. **Allocate to tenants**
6. **Tap "Save"**

Owner will receive notification about the new bill.

#### Submitting Expenses

When you pay for maintenance or repairs:

1. **Go to "Expenses"** tab
2. **Tap "Add Expense"**
3. **Fill in details**
4. **Upload receipt**
5. **Mark "Requires Approval"**
6. **Tap "Submit"**

Owner will receive notification to approve or reject.

### Managing Tenants

You can:

- ✅ Create new tenants
- ✅ View tenant information
- ✅ Record payments
- ✅ Send messages
- ❌ Edit rent amounts (owner only)
- ❌ Deactivate tenants (owner only)

### Working Offline

The app works offline! Changes are saved locally and synced when connection is restored.

**Offline capabilities**:

- Record payments
- Create bills
- Add expenses
- View tenant information
- Access payment history

**Auto-sync**:

- Every 15 minutes when online
- Immediately when recording critical data
- Manual sync available in settings

#### Resolving Sync Conflicts

If you and the owner edit the same data offline:

1. **You'll see "Sync Conflict" notification**
2. **Open "Sync Conflicts"** from menu
3. **Review both versions**:
   - Your version
   - Server version
4. **Choose which to keep**:
   - Keep yours
   - Keep server version
   - Merge both (if possible)
5. **Tap "Resolve"**

As a tenant, you can view your lease details, payment history, pay rent online, and communicate with your landlord.

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard shows:

- **Monthly Rent**
- **Current Balance**
- **Next Payment Due**

2. **View**:
   - Property information
   - Unit number
   - Move-in date

#### Paying Rent Online

1. **Go to "Payments"** tab
2. **Tap "Pay Rent"**
3. **Verify amount**
4. **Select payment gateway**:
   - **Khalti** (Digital wallet)
   - **eSewa** (Digital wallet)
   - **IME Pay** (Bank payment)
5. **Tap "Pay Now"**
6. **Complete payment** in gateway app/website
7. **Return to MeroGhar** for confirmation

You'll receive:

- Payment confirmation notification
- Email receipt
- Updated balance in app

#### Viewing Payment History

1. **Go to "Payments"** tab
2. **View all payments** with:
   - Date
   - Amount
   - Payment method
   - Receipt
3. **Tap on payment** to view/download receipt

#### Exporting Payment History

For personal records or tax purposes:

1. **Go to "Payments"** tab
2. **Tap "Export"**
3. **Select date range**
4. **Choose format**:

#### Monthly Utility Bills

2. **View your bill shares**:

#### Paying Bill Shares

Bills are usually added to your next rent payment. If you want to pay separately:

1. **Go to "Documents"**

## Troubleshooting

### Login Issues

**"Invalid credentials" error**:

- Verify email and password are correct
- Check if Caps Lock is on
- Use "Forgot Password" to reset

**"Account not found"**:

- Verify you registered with this email
- Check your email for verification link
- Contact support if problem persists

### Sync Issues

**"Sync failed" error**:

- Check internet connection
- Try manual sync from settings
- Restart app
- If problem persists, contact support

**Sync conflicts**:

- Go to "Sync Conflicts" in menu
- Review conflicting data
- Choose which version to keep
- Resolve conflict

### Payment Issues

**Online payment failed**:

- Verify payment gateway account has sufficient balance
- Check if payment gateway is working
- Try different payment method
- Contact payment gateway support

**Receipt not generated**:

- Check "Payments" tab for payment record
- If payment exists, tap "Resend Receipt"
- Check email spam folder
- Contact support if receipt not received

### Notification Issues

**Not receiving notifications**:

- Check notification permissions in device settings
- Verify notification settings in app
- Check Do Not Disturb mode on device
- Try logging out and back in

### Performance Issues

**App is slow**:

- Close other apps
- Clear app cache in settings
- Restart device
- Update to latest version

**App crashes**:

- Update to latest version
- Clear app data (warning: will require re-login)
- Reinstall app
- Report crash to support with details

## FAQs

### General

**Q: Is MeroGhar free to use?**
A: MeroGhar offers a free tier for up to 5 tenants. Paid plans available for larger portfolios. Check our website for pricing.

**Q: Can I use MeroGhar offline?**
A: Yes! Most features work offline and sync automatically when connection is restored.

**Q: Which devices are supported?**
A: MeroGhar works on Android 8.0+ and iOS 12.0+.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. We use bank-level encryption (AES-256) and all data is transmitted over HTTPS. We never share your data with third parties.

### For Owners

**Q: Can I manage multiple properties?**
A: Yes, there's no limit on the number of properties you can add.

**Q: Can I have multiple intermediaries?**
A: Yes, you can assign different intermediaries to different properties with custom permissions.

**Q: How do I export data for my accountant?**
A: Go to Reports → Select report type → Tap "Share" → Generate link. Share the link with your accountant.

**Q: Can I customize receipt templates?**
A: Not yet, but this feature is coming soon! Currently, receipts use a standard template with your property information.

### For Tenants

**Q: Do I need to pay to use the app?**
A: No, tenants can use the app completely free.

**Q: Are there fees for online payments?**
A: Payment gateway fees apply (usually 2-3%). Your landlord's policy determines who pays the fee.

**Q: Can I pay partial rent?**
A: Yes, if your landlord allows it. The app tracks your balance automatically.

**Q: How do I get my security deposit back?**
A: Contact your landlord directly. Security deposit refunds are handled outside the app.

### For Intermediaries

**Q: Can I manage properties for multiple owners?**
A: Yes, you can be assigned to properties from multiple owners. Each will appear in your property list.

**Q: What happens if I make changes offline?**
A: Changes are saved locally and synced automatically when connection is restored. In case of conflicts, you'll be notified to resolve them.

**Q: Can I send messages to tenants?**
A: Yes, you have the same messaging capabilities as owners for properties you manage.

## Glossary

- **Owner**: Property owner with full control over properties and tenants
- **Intermediary**: Property manager who handles daily operations
- **Tenant**: Person renting a unit
- **RLS**: Row-Level Security (ensures you only see your own data)
- **Bill Allocation**: Dividing utility bills among tenants
- **Sync**: Synchronizing data between device and server
- **Receipt**: Official payment confirmation document
- **GST**: Goods and Services Tax (13% in Nepal)
- **Security Deposit**: Refundable deposit collected at move-in
